EXPLORE VALPETRA

Nestled in the heights of Verbier , this Chalet is one of the most prestigious Swiss chalets, located in the much sought-after Alpine setting of the Valais resort.

Spa & Relaxation

An indoor swimming pool, the colour of which is reminiscent of mountain lakes, facing the giants of the Alps revealed by the large windows. A bar with a relaxation area lined with a dozen deckchairs, a lounge area, a fitness room with a view of the outside, a sauna, a hamman, and a massage room.

Breathtaking view

The view of the Combins and Mont-Blanc massif, overlooking the Vallée de Bagnes, is magnificent.

Comfort & Wellness

A massage room dedicated to well-being, fulfilling all expectations of absolute comfort.

Events & Local Specialties

LAYOUT OF THE CHALET
and the amazing features
A true joy of the Alps and a promise of absolute luxury, this Chalet dominates the resort with its presence, its imposing architecture and its top-of-the-range materials.
With its three chalets linked by an underground funicular, its 3520 m2 of sales area, its plot of almost 5,000 m2 and its exceptional environment perched above Verbier, the chalet blends in with its Alpine setting in complete elegance and discretion.
