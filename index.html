EXPLORE VALPETRA

Nestled in the heights of Verbier , this Chalet is one of the most prestigious Swiss chalets, located in the much sought-after Alpine setting of the Valais resort.

IMPRESSIONS
from the chalet
LUXURY

in the heart of the mountains

Indoor Pool

An indoor swimming pool, the colour of which is reminiscent of mountain lakes, facing the giants of the Alps revealed by the large windows.

Breathtaking view

The view of the Combins and Mont-Blanc massif, overlooking the Vallée de Bagnes, is magnificent.

Comfort & Relaxation

A massage room and so much more on a floor dedicated to well-being, fulfilling all expectations of absolute luxury.

Journey of Discovery

A cave whose sole purpose is to transport you through the chalet, but which will take you on a journey of discovery…

Entertainment

An immersive cinema room where time stands still, giving way to new dimensions.

Fun & Recreation

Aside from a fitness room with a view of the outside, a sauna, and a hamman, we also offer a space for fun and socialisation.

LAYOUT OF THE CHALET
and the amazing features
01
A true joy of the Alps and a promise of absolute luxury, this Chalet dominates the resort with its presence, its imposing architecture and its top-of-the-range materials.
02
With its three chalets linked by an underground funicular, its 3520 m2 of sales area, its plot of almost 5,000 m2 and its exceptional environment perched above Verbier, the chalet blends in with its Alpine setting in complete elegance and discretion.
01
The Valpetra chalet has many treasures in store, such as a carnotzet with a raclette oven, a wine cellar, an immersive cinema room, and a cave which will take you on a journey of discovery.
02
The spa includes an indoor swimming pool, the colour of which is reminiscent of mountain lakes, facing the giants of the Alps revealed by the large windows.
03
A bar with a relaxation area, a lounge area, a fitness room with a view of the outside, a sauna, a hamman, a massage room and an ice machine.
